Antoine Fuqua subverted the traditional rap video by stripping away the party to shoot a dark, brooding cinematic confrontation between Coolio and Michelle Pfeiffer — a direct promotional tie-in for Dangerous Minds that brought Hollywood-level lighting to a hip-hop promo.
