The White Stripes' "Seven Nation Army" is a direct visual translation of its iconic, driving riff. Directed by Alex and Martin, the video's hypnotic, forward-plunging tunnel was a digital illusion created by compositing green screen footage of the band into an animated, kaleidoscopic world. It's a pure fusion of the band's minimalist sound and their strict black, white, and red visual identity.
