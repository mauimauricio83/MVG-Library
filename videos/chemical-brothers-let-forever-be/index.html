The Chemical Brothers' "Let Forever Be" is a mind-bending illusion built on pure physical craft. At a time when digital effects were taking over, director Michel Gondry went the other way. He treated his dancers as the special effect, choreographing them into human pixels to create the kaleidoscopic patterns and digital glitches you see on screen. Every surreal transition and visual trail was captured live in-camera.
