The gritty, high-energy look of Wolfmother's "Woman" video was created using a photocopier. Directors Alex & Martin, famous for "Seven Nation Army," used a unique technique of photocopying every frame of footage to achieve its raw, exhilarating effect. It's a perfect visual match for a band that recorded their debut album in the same legendary studio as Nirvana's "Nevermind." 🎨 The album's iconic cover art was created by the legendary fantasy illustrator Frank Frazetta. 🇦🇺 When they won for "Woman," Wolfmother became the first Australian band to win a Grammy in 25 years, with Men At Work being the last to do so. 📺 The song was prominently featured in an episode of the hit TV show "The O.C.,"
