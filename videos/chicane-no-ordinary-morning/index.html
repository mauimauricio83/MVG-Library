Released in July 2000 as the third single from Behind the Sun, with vocals by Tracy Ackerman, and directed by John Hillcoat years before The Proposition, The Road, and Lawless. A messy rights dispute between Chicane and label Xtravaganza beginning in 2002 left the original master hard to license — and the official video largely vanished from the internet with it, surviving mainly as re-ups with TV channel logos burned into the corner.
