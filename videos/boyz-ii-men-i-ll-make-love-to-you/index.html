Genre

I'll Make Love to You tied the all-time record for most weeks at number one on the Billboard Hot 100 at the time — 14 weeks, matching Whitney Houston's "I Will Always Love You" — and won Best R&B Video at the 1994 VMAs. With the follow-up "On Bended Knee," Boyz II Men became the first act since The Beatles in 1964 to chart consecutive number-one singles in the United States.
