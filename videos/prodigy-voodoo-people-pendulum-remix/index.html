The strange, blindfolded race in the video for "Voodoo People (Pendulum Remix)" was inspired by a movie. Director Ron Scalpello based the concept on a scene from the 2001 Spanish thriller "Intacto," a film about people who compete in underground games of luck. To add another layer of insider trivia, the woman who wins the brutal race is Sharky, one of the original female members of The Prodigy from their early days.
