Will Smith walked into the 1997 VMAs with the biggest movie of the year strapped to his back and walked out with Best Video from a Film. A music video in blockbuster clothes — slick, massive, built to win — it earned four nominations, including Best Male Video, Best Choreography, and Best Special Effects.
