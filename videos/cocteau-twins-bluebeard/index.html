Cocteau Twins' "Bluebeard" is a dreamscape brought to life, directed by Paul Donnellon. Donnellon has shared behind-the-scenes stories from the set, including that he showed up with his own made-up lyrics, and that an on-set mishap sent singer Liz Fraser flying off a turntable.
