Shot frame-by-frame with hand-recranked film exposures, "Crystalline" turns a meteor shower into a cosmic conductor's set piece — Björk as a lunar goddess overseeing the collision of light and rhythm. It closes the Björk/Gondry cycle that began with "Human Behaviour" in 1993.
