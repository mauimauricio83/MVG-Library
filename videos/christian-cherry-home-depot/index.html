Christian is an emerging alternative artist from Cyprus whose work threads together faith, desire, and queer identity. Raised in a country where the church and media still police sexuality, Home Depot sits right in that tension, delivered with a sonic palette that pulls from singer-songwriters like Elliott Smith, Lana Del Rey, Phoebe Bridgers.
