Frames

Chuengue Project

Director
Lucas Rangel e Pedro Knoll
Year
2026
Category
Music Video

Synopsis "Frames" projects memories like vintage film reels, utilizing an experimental, high-contrast aesthetic that blends past and present. The visual narrative alternates between slow, contemplative shots and vibrant flashes—which intensify into a rapid whirlwind as the music builds—suggesting the shedding of time’s constraints and the beauty of existential ephemerality. The piece integrates scenes of the band performing, creating a dynamic contrast that reflects on perception, the subconscious, and the desire to capture the present in fleeting moments.
