Stockholm Syndrome is an alternative rock track blending folk and Midwest emo influences. It explores the thin line between love, obsession, and projection through the story of becoming attached not to a person, but to the idea of them. The song captures the comfort of fantasy, the pain of disillusionment, and the difficulty of letting go of an imagined relationship.
