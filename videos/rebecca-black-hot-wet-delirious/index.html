"Hot Wet Delirious" is the second single from Rebecca Black's upcoming album, following "Speakerphone." This time she co-directed alongside Hannah De Vries, a real step up in creative control. The video plays out as a decadent dinner party unraveling into chaos, with Rebecca cast as the hotel maid moving through it all — serving tray in hand, weaving through guests covered in cake and wine. The styling leans hard into BDSM imagery: leather and lace, restraints, a black feathered hat that repeatedly drops over her face like a veil before lifting again. What's interesting is the power dynamic. She's playing the maid — technically the one serving, technically the one in chains — but every frame she's in, she's the one directing the room's attention. The song itself is about crossing a line you know you shouldn't. The video makes that literal.
