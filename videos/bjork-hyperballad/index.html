Björk's "Hyperballad" is a visual poem about emotional self-preservation. Director Michel Gondry translated the song's intimate lyrics into a dreamscape, combining a video-game world with a live performance. Using complex in-camera techniques, the team layered eight different exposures onto the same piece of film, creating a surreal blend of the digital and the real. It's a technical feat that perfectly captures the song's raw and beautiful message.
