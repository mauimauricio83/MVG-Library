Freak on a Leash

Korn

Director
Todd McFarlane & Jonathan Dayton & Valerie Faris
Year
1999
Category
Music Video
Genre
Hard Rock
Country
United States

Todd McFarlane directed the animated intro and outro, while Dayton and Faris handled the live-action segments. High-speed Photo-Sonics cameras shooting thousands of frames per second captured real cookie jars and lava lamps being destroyed on a soundstage (the bullet itself was CG), and the band performed in a room with a thousand bullet holes creating the "god ray" lighting that tracks the projectile's path. It won the 2000 Grammy for Best Short Form Music Video.
