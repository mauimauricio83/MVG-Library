There are three official videos for Alanis Morissette's "Head Over Feet". The most famous version is a single, unbroken close-up on her face. An alternate cut even exists where she breaks into laughter at the end, a "blooper" that adds a layer of raw, unfiltered joy to the video. The song is widely believed to be about actor Dash Mihok, who Alanis was dating at the time and who also appeared in her "Ironic" video. "Head Over Feet" was the fifth single from the massive album Jagged Little Pill, which won the Grammy for Album of the Year. The song became her first to hit #1 on the Billboard Adult Top 40 chart in the U.S., showing her appeal beyond alternative rock radio.
