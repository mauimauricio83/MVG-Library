Blue Orchid by The White Stripes is a gothic fairytale from the mind of legendary director Sigismondi, Floria. A contemporary of directors like Michel Gondry, Sigismondi created a surreal and unsettling world starring model Karen Elson, who met her future husband, Jack White, on the set. The video is a masterclass in visual symbolism, exploring themes of temptation and power that are made even more intense by the real-life romance that began during its creation.
