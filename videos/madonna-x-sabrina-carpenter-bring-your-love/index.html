Bring Your Love anchors Confessions II: The Film — a thirteen-minute, six-track continuous piece directed by TORSO (David Toro and Solomon Chase), produced by DIVISION and supported by Dolce & Gabbana. It premiered at the Tribeca Film Festival on June 5, 2026 before going public on YouTube on June 8. Madonna, at the Tribeca Q&A moderated by Anderson Cooper: "I like the idea of film. Somehow video seems cheap. It was good when it was just MTV and me." The Confessions II album followed on July 3.
