Sophie Muller filmed the messy, behind-the-scenes garage-band reality of a group actively fighting with each other on set while the media focused entirely on their lead singer. The awkward tension was real — Gwen Stefani and bassist Tony Kanal had recently ended a seven-year relationship — and Muller deliberately framed the male band members out of focus or at the edge of the screen to mirror the band's actual frustrations with press coverage.
