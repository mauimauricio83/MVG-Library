O-Zone's "Dragostea Din Tei" is a joyous, baffling piece of pop culture with two competing visual legacies: the official video, with the band dancing on the wing of an airplane, and Gary Brolsma's legendary "Numa Numa" webcam video, which turned the song into a viral internet anthem.
