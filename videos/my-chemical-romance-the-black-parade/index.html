The Black Parade

My Chemical Romance

Director
Samuel Bayer
Year
2006
Category
Music Video
Genre
Alternative
Country
United States

The elaborate costumes worn by the band were designed by Colleen Atwood, the multi-Oscar-winning costume designer famous for her frequent collaborations with Tim Burton. The shoot was physically grueling. Drummer Bob Bryar received a third-degree burn on his leg that developed a staph infection, and Gerard Way tore ligaments in his ankle. The video was filmed at a rented space shuttle hangar outside of Downey, California
