Let Me Ride won Best Rap Video at the 1994 VMAs and served as the visual calling card of The Chronic — the album that restructured what West Coast hip hop looked like, sounded like, and sold like. Dr. Dre in a convertible, low riders, and a G-funk aesthetic that became the template for a decade of rap visuals. The song samples Parliament's "Mothership Connection," and the video's low-rider visual language directly references 1970s Parliament-Funkadelic album art.
