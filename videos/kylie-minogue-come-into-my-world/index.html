Kylie Minogue's "Come Into My World" is a technical marvel directed by Michel Gondry. What appears to be a single, continuous shot is actually a complex illusion created by layering multiple takes on top of each other. Using a motion-control camera to ensure identical camera movement for each pass, Gondry crafted a perfectly timed, surrealist loop that stands as one of the most innovative and meticulously planned videos ever made.
