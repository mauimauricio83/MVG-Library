The Chemical Brothers' "Elektrobank" is a masterclass in creative contrast. Instead of matching the song's electronic chaos, director Spike Jonze tells the quiet, human story of a gymnast—played by future director Sofia Coppola—on the day of a competition. The video uses the intense track as a soundtrack for her internal state, creating a powerful and unexpected emotional depth.
