Shot and Edited by Maui Mauricio Starring - Jess Foenander, Luke Swann, Lara Patzel & Mike Foenander Song Written, Recorded, Mixed and Mastered by Jess Foenander The music video "Funky Drummer" was conceived, filmed and delivered in 10 days for the 10x10 initiative presented by Channel 44 and the Adelaide Fringe. Originally conceived by initiative indie filmmaker Norwood Cheek, 10x10 pairs up aspiring local musical artists with film making teams to create 10 videos in 10 days.
