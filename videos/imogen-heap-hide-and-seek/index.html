The first intentional vertical music video. Director Joel Peissig shot Imogen Heap's 2005 "Hide and Seek" on 35mm film rotated sideways, saying the portrait frame suited her face and her solitude — and that the rotation produced better light streaks. The format went unnoticed for years, long before vertical became the dominant frame in music video.
