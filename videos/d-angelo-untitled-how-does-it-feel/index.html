D'Angelo's "Untitled" is a masterclass in vulnerability and minimalism. Director Paul Hunter created the illusion of a single take that focuses entirely on the artist's raw performance. The video's massive success had a profound and lasting impact on his career, creating an iconic image that would define a moment in music history. The world was heartbroken to learn of his passing on October 14, 2025, but the incredible legacy of this neo-soul pioneer will never be forgotten.
