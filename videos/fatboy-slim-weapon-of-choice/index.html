Christopher Walken has been a professionally trained dancer since he was a child. In the "Weapon of Choice" video, he performed the vast majority of his own dance moves. For the more dangerous acrobatics and some of the flying sequences, a stunt double was used. But the iconic style and unforgettable performance are all Walken. The flying itself was accomplished with practical wirework, not CGI.
