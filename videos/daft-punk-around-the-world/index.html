Daft Punk's "Around the World" is a masterclass in conceptual design. Faced with a highly repetitive track, director Michel Gondry created a living blueprint of the song's composition. Each group of dancers represents a specific instrument, turning a simple loop into an endlessly fascinating visual machine where you can see how every single musical layer interacts.
