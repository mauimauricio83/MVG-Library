Keep Tryin'

Utada Hikaru

Director
Kazuaki Kiriya
Year
2005
Category
Music Video
Genre
J-Pop

Hikaru Utada's "Keep Tryin'" is a monument to creative ambition and logistical planning. Directed by Kazuaki Kiriya, the video's vibrant world was brought to life through dozens of elaborate, handmade practical sets. Its most technically demanding moments are the brilliant match cuts, where Utada's character and costume change instantly while her motion continues flawlessly. This required incredible physical discipline to make the edit invisible. It's a video that perfectly embodies its own theme—a marathon of performance and precision, showcasing an artist's commitment to a grand creative vision.
