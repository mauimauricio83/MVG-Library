Lenny Kravitz's "Are You Gonna Go My Way" was an exercise in minimalist design and technical innovation. Director Mark Romanek centered the entire video around one unforgettable set piece: a massive chandelier made of nearly 1,000 individually controlled aquarium lights. This element, designed by Michael Keeling, gave the video its iconic, futuristic look and perfectly captured the song's raw energy. The video went on to win an MTV Video Music Award and helped define the look of 90s rock.
