Coco Lee's 1999 breakout for the Runaway Bride soundtrack made her the first Asian-American artist to top the MTV Asia Hitlist — then copyright battles between Sony and the film studio over the Julia Roberts footage kept the high-quality video offline for over two decades, surviving only as low-quality fan uploads. In early 2025, Sony Music Taiwan finally released an official 4K restoration, a poignant posthumous tribute to the singer, who died in 2023.
