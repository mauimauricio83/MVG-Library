Candu Baru

Zat kimia

Director
Esa Bani
Year
2018
Category
Music Video

Music video employs a distinct, surreal collage animation style, blending retro aesthetic elements, old newspaper clippings, analog technology motifs, and vintage archival footage. This stylistic contrast highlights the friction between the organic human experience and the synthetic "new addiction" of our interconnected digital society.
