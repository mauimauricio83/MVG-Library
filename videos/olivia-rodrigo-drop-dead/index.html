Petra Collins secured the Palace of Versailles, then shot it like a vintage home movie — 4:3 aspect ratio with PAL 50i interlacing, mimicking the exact broadcast standards of 1980s European television — grounding a royal palace in intimate, nostalgic memory that matches the song's lyrical nod to The Cure's "Just Like Heaven." The single debuted at number one on the Hot 100, making Rodrigo the first artist to debut the lead single from each of her first three albums at number one.
