This music video was made by aivilO (Olivia Wuilbaut) and Valentine Jaffe. It was filmed in Brussels and Tervuren. The song 'wild butterfly' expresses the acceptance of the versatility of life, the fragility of life, like the fragility of a wild butterfly. This song features one word in Dutch: 'wispelturig'. This means 'fickle' and is the aspect of life that is being explored in this song.
