Scream

M. Jackson/J. Jackson

Director
Mark Romanek
Year
1995
Category
Music Video

Michael and Janet Jackson's "Scream" was a 7-million-dollar statement. Officially the most expensive music video ever made, director Mark Romanek built an entire self-contained world for the siblings to escape the pressures of fame. With thirteen custom-built sets, the futuristic spaceship became a symbol of their defiance against the media. The video's legacy includes a Grammy, multiple VMAs, and a permanent spot in pop culture history.
