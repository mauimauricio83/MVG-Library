Head Back

Faintin' Goats

Director
Del Donno, Alessio
Year
2020
Category
Music Video

The video is representative of the sonic and iconoclastic chaos of the dadaist-duo "Faintin' Goats," who, with a mix of punk, electronic, and industrial, draws on the imagery of "found footage" and visual-psychedelia to remix the meanings of content, stolen clips and old advertisings, re-semanticizing them. The work is imbued with noise, of course, and the video took great care to pay attention to the smallest details to best connect with the music. It's a creative work driven to some extent by instinct, as the director and editor is also the duo's singer and guitarist. The visual violence accompanies the sonic violence, and careful viewing is not recommended for those with epilepsy...
