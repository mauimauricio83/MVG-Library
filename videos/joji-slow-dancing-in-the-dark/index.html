Joji's "SLOW DANCING IN THE DARK" symbolizes a tragic conflict from Greek myth. He's depicted as a Pan-like satyr, a figure of wild nature, who has been fatally wounded by Cupid's arrow of romantic love. The video suggests his very nature makes the relationship's tragic end inevitable, a powerful statement in his transition to a serious musical artist.
