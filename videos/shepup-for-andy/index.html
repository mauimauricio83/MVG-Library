A queer love song written as a manifesto, “For Andy” is an indie-folk confession to a newly discovered sapphic relationship. Built around acoustic guitar, harp, bass, and understated drums, shepup surprised her partner by debuting the song live as a gift. Warm, whimsical, and disarmingly sincere, “For Andy” pairs narcotic vocal melodies with vivid declarations of devotion, turning private dreams into something universal. The music video was captured on 16mm scanned to digital.
