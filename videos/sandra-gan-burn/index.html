Local indie singer/songwriter Sandra Gan joined 3 local hip-hop dancers and a team of UniSA staff and students to create a music video for her song "Burn". The music video "Burn" was conceived, filmed and delivered in 10 days for the 10x10 initiative presented by Channel 44 and the Adelaide Fringe. Originally conceived by initiative indie filmmaker Norwood Cheek, 10x10 pairs up aspiring local musical artists with film making teams to create 10 videos in 10 days.
