Romain Gavras turned a futuristic British boarding school into a feral, adult-free dystopia — a modern Lord of the Flies with Yung Lean as its leader, tracked in continuous, visceral camera motion that erupts into a massive practical Dance choreographed by Damien Jalet. The seven-and-a-half-minute film merges the tracks "Storm I" and "Storm II"; Gener8ion is the electronic moniker of French producer Surkin, and the chaos is exactly what you'd expect from the director of M.I.A.'s "Bad Girls" and Jamie xx's "Gosh."
