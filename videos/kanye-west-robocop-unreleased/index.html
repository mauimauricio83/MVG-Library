RoboCop (unreleased)

Kanye West

Director
Hype Williams
Year
2008
Category
Music Video

Kanye West's stop-motion video starring then-girlfriend Amber Rose, shot in 2008 to promote 808s & Heartbreak, was shelved after their high-profile breakup and considered lost media for 16 years. In late 2024, multiple versions surfaced on YouTube — uploaded, it was later revealed, by the video's own VFX supervisor Stephan Zlotescu — including an "Amber Cut" with Rose moving like a stop-motion robot and a "Doll Cut" replacing her with a mannequin head. The leaked filename, robocop-v5_HD, suggests it was the fifth edit before abandonment.
