UNKLE's "Rabbit in Your Headlights" is a harrowing short film. Director Jonathan Glazer created a hypnotic, brutalist loop, starring actor Denis Lavant as a man who simply refuses to stay down. The direction was simple: "play dead" after each impact, making every recovery a shock to the system. The result is a masterpiece of tension, a relentless cycle of punishment and endurance that culminates in a moment of surreal, explosive transcendence. It’s a video that tests the viewer, blurring the line between music promo and art film.
