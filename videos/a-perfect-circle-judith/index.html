A Perfect Circle's "Judith" is a masterclass in minimalism, directed by David Fincher. The video's stark, performance-only concept was a deliberate choice to amplify the song's intensely personal subject matter: frontman Maynard James Keenan's direct conflict with the unwavering faith of his mother, Judith, in the face of a paralysis that lasted for 27 years. By stripping away all narrative, the video becomes a powerful vessel for the raw, unfiltered emotion of the song itself.
